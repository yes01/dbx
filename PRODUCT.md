# Product

## Register

product

## Users

TestTeam engineers, analysts, and operators who manage database connections, inspect schemas, run SQL, move data, compare structures, and use internal AI-assisted workflows inside a desktop environment. They are often in focused work sessions where trust, speed, and clear state feedback matter more than decoration.

## Product Purpose

DBX is an internal database management client for TestTeam workflows. It brings connection management, query execution, data browsing, import/export, comparison, transfer utilities, driver management, and policy-controlled AI assistance into one dependable desktop tool. Success means users can understand where they are, act quickly, and trust the interface when working with production-adjacent data.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, capable. The product should feel like paid professional infrastructure software: quiet, dense, legible, and confident, with enough color to encode meaning without becoming ornamental.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS styling: purple-blue gradient surfaces, oversized rounded cards, glassy decoration, marketing-style hero layouts, empty card grids, and low-contrast gray text. Avoid hobby-tool visuals that make destructive or data-moving actions feel casual.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize operational clarity over spectacle.
- Use color as information: connection identity, object type, status, and current selection.
- Keep the shell calm so editors, tables, and dialogs can carry task detail.
- Make common workflows dense but scannable, with consistent controls and stable panel geometry.
- Preserve familiar desktop affordances while raising polish through tokens, spacing, contrast, and states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Maintain keyboard-visible focus states, reduced-motion compatibility, color-independent status cues, and comfortable legibility across English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and Spanish UI text.
